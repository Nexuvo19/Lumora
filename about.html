Our Journey

Building Legacies Since 2007

Lumora Estates was born from a simple yet powerful belief: that every family deserves a home that inspires. Founded in 2007 by Aryan Mehra in Mumbai's bustling Bandra Kurla Complex, we started with a single promise — to create luxury spaces that don't just house people, but elevate lives.

What began as a boutique real estate consultancy has evolved into India's most celebrated luxury property brand, with a presence across 12 cities, a portfolio of over 850 properties, and the trust of more than 12,000 families who call our creations home.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the world's most admired luxury real estate brand — one that transforms the art of living by creating spaces where architectural excellence, sustainable design, and human connection converge in perfect harmony.

We envision a future where every Lumora home is a benchmark of quality that inspires communities and endures across generations.

Our Mission

To deliver extraordinary real estate experiences by combining impeccable design, transparent processes, and personalized service — ensuring every client finds not just a property, but a true reflection of their aspirations.

We commit to building with integrity, innovating with purpose, and serving with heart — every single day.

Milestones

Our Journey Through Time

2007
The Beginning
Lumora Estates founded in Mumbai with a team of 5 and a bold vision to redefine luxury real estate in India.
2010
First Landmark Project
Delivered Lumora Residences — our first 100-unit premium apartment project in South Mumbai, fully sold out in 3 weeks.
2014
Pan-India Expansion
Expanded to Delhi, Bangalore, and Hyderabad. Crossed 500 property milestone and won our first Forbes Real Estate Award.
2018
International Footprint
Launched Lumora International with projects in Dubai and Singapore. Crossed ₹10,000 Cr in total sales value.
2021
Digital Transformation
Launched AI-powered property matching, 3D virtual tours, and digital conveyancing — a first in Indian luxury real estate.
2025
New Heights
850+ properties, 12,000+ happy families, 100+ awards, and the launch of Lumora Grand City — our most ambitious project yet.
