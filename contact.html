FAQ

Common Questions

What is the process for buying a property with Lumora?
+

Our process is simple: Initial consultation → Property selection → Legal due diligence → Agreement to Sell → Loan processing (if needed) → Registration → Key handover. Our team guides you at every step.

Do you assist NRI (Non-Resident Indian) buyers?
+

Absolutely! We have a dedicated NRI Services team that handles everything remotely — from virtual tours to documentation, FEMA compliance, and power of attorney arrangements. You can complete your purchase without visiting India.

Are home loans available for Lumora properties?
+

Yes! We have tie-ups with 20+ leading banks and NBFCs offering preferential interest rates for Lumora buyers. Our finance team will help you get the best loan terms.

Is there a brokerage fee for buying a property?
+

For most developer projects, there's no brokerage fee for buyers — we're compensated by the developers. For resale properties, a nominal fee of 1–2% is applicable. This will be clearly disclosed before you commit.
